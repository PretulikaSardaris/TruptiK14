Hi!

Dr. Trupti is a remarkable dentist hailing from the vibrant city of Thane, India.

Dr. Trupti's passion for holistic well-being led her to pursue a Masters in Yoga. This unique combination of dental expertise and yoga mastery allows her to approach oral health from a comprehensive perspective, understanding the interconnectedness of the body, mind, and mouth.

These experiences enhance her ability to connect with people from all walks of life, creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere in her dental practice. Dr. Trupti's dedication to dentistry, combined with her passion for travel and expertise in yoga, makes her a unique and inspiring healthcare professional.
Whether she's transforming smiles or exploring the world, she continuously seeks to make a positive impact on the lives of those around her.

Places I recommend to visit!




During my trip to Umberkhind, I was captivated by the natural beauty that surrounded me. The picturesque valley nestled amidst lush green hills provided a serene and peaceful ambiance.
Trekking through the enchanting trails, I encountered breathtaking waterfalls and heard the melodious sounds of chirping birds. The rich biodiversity of the region fascinated me, with vibrant flora and fauna dotting the landscape. As I reached the summit, I was rewarded with panoramic views that left me in awe of Umberkhind's mesmerizing charm.

Embarking on a trek to Chanderi Fort was a thrilling and adventurous experience. The ancient fort, perched atop a rugged hill, offered a mesmerizing blend of history and scenic beauty.
The challenging ascent tested my stamina and determination, but the stunning vistas that unfolded along the way made it all worthwhile. Upon reaching the fort, I was greeted by magnificent ruins and architectural marvels that spoke volumes about its glorious past. The panoramic views from the fort's vantage point showcased the breathtaking landscape, leaving an indelible impression of Chanderi's grandeur in my heart.

My trek to Kalavantin Durga was an exhilarating adventure filled with adrenaline and stunning views. The steep and rugged trail challenged my physical endurance, but the thrill of conquering the mighty fort made it all worthwhile. As I ascended, the panoramic vistas of the surrounding Sahyadri mountains unfolded before my eyes, leaving me awe-inspired. The ancient fort's intricate architecture and historical significance added a sense of intrigue to the experience. Standing atop Kalavantin Durga,
I felt a profound sense of accomplishment and a deep connection with the majestic beauty of nature..

AND LAST BUT NOT LEAST MY MOST FAVORITE!!
Embarking on a trek to Kalsubai, the highest peak in Maharashtra, was a remarkable and exclusive adventure.
The journey to the summit challenged both my physical and mental limits as I traversed rugged terrains and steep slopes. The breathtaking views along the way, including lush green valleys and cascading waterfalls, provided a surreal backdrop. Reaching the pinnacle rewarded me with a sense of triumph and a panoramic vista that stretched as far as the eye could see. The serene atmosphere atop Kalsubai created a spiritual connection with nature,
leaving an indelible mark on my soul. This exclusive experience allowed me to witness the grandeur of the Sahyadri mountain range and instilled a deep appreciation for the raw beauty of the natural world.

I witness the power of yoga in my improved posture, increased energy levels, and enhanced focus throughout the day. It allows me to quiet the noise of the external world, cultivating mindfulness and deepening my connection to the present moment.
